Programmer MBTI

  • Older research found ISTJ to be most common
  • Also quite common personality type in US in general
  • Research correlated quite a lot with general indicators

Programmer MBTI

  • Newer research found INTJ to be most common
  • Very different from general population
  • Other common types: ISTJ, ENTJ, INFJ, INTP

Shift from ISTJ to INTJ

  • Intuition over Sensing
  • Why this shift?

Shift from ISTJ to INTJ

  • Intuition over Sensing
  • Why this shift?

Sensing

  • Sensing is considered connected to "sequential learning"
  • Spend some years in university learning the science
  • Start working with it

Modern World

  • "The Science" has become too complex to learn sequentially
  • Many new technologies coming and disappearing
  • Whole paradigms shifting
  • Learning different things rapidly required

Intuition

  • Intuition types are connected to "global learning"
  • Skimming through material until they "get it"
  • Gaining fast understanding for underlying concepts
  • Able to solve problems with new technologies faster

My "Research"

  • Relatively small sample size
  • Working in different companies with different languages
  • Working in very different areas of industry
  • Mostly people I know and respect

Trends

  • I was slightly more common than E (introverts over extroverts)
  • All programmers were N (intuition over sensing)
  • T was slightly more common than F (thinking over feeling)
  • J was more common than P (judging over perceiving)

INTJ

  • Average developer type
  • Correlates with latest research

INTJ Profile

  • "The Scientist"
  • Observe the world and generate new ideas
  • Learn new concepts really fast
  • Want to implement ideas not just theorize about them
  • Good strategists

Bugger-all

Nothing

People are different

Relatively few are actually INTJ

  • According to different sources 15 - 30% of devs are INTJ
  • In my experiment, I only found couple of INTJ people

Indicators are not binary

  • Almost nobody is 100% introvert or extrovert
  • Most people fall somewhere between only slightly tilting towards one

Traits can be learned

  • Although introverts may be less social, they can train to be more social
  • Extroverts can train to think more introvertedly

INTJ does not actually mean best

  • All personalities have their strengths and weaknesses
  • The versatile world of what programming is now really relies on them

INFP

  • Lot of developers I really respect are INFP
  • Not very common IT personality
  • More often musicians or writers
  • Likes to dream and cares for greater good
  • Often good teachers and diplomats

We are all people

  • We have our own strengths
  • We have our own weaknesses
  • We have different motivations
  • And nobody is perfect
  • Although sometimes we think we are...

Developers have many weaknesses

  • We might think we are smarter than we really are
  • We might get too excited about some irrelevant things
  • We might get distracted by some less relevant details
  • We might lose the touch with reality
  • We might get depressed by the work.

TEAM

Building a team

  • Not the easiest tasks
  • Can't put bunch of INTJ's together in same room and expect great results
  • They probably kill each other.

Great team

  • Is versatile
  • Different people who complete each other
  • Can have productive discussions
  • Make decisions with consensus
  • Help each other whenever one is stuck
  • Become friends.
Not only devs but also testers, managers, etc.

Molecule of development

  • When individuals are atoms the team is the molecule
  • Atoms are useful and all, but in reality we truly need them molecules
  • Before trying to analyze people, analyze teams
  • If team is not working, figure out why not
  • If team is working, try to not break it
  • If it is breaking, try to fix it
  • Never discard the teams as irrelevant!
